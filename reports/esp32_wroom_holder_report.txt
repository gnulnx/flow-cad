Erb ESP32-WROOM holder CAD report
==================================

Working board envelope:
- Board assumed: 28.5 W x 55.0 L mm
- Internal case envelope: 31.7 W x 58.2 L mm

Final outer dimensions:
- Base body: 42.0 W x 83.0 L x 15.0 H mm
- Lid: 37.3 W x 82.3 L x 4.6 H mm
- Closed assembly: 42.0 W x 83.0 L x 17.6 H mm

Access features:
- Side pin openings: 52.0 L x 16.0 H mm on both long sides
- Bottom pin troughs: 6.2 W x 52.0 L mm below both header rows
- USB opening: 12.5 W x 7.0 H mm on one short end
- IMU/cable opening: 18.0 W x 7.0 H mm on the opposite short end
- Lid antenna slots: 4 slots, each 4.0 W x 24.0 L mm

Screw sizes assumed:
- Chassis/shelf mount: M3 clearance holes, 3.4 mm
- Lid screws: M2.5/M2.6 clearance holes, 2.8 mm, pilot holes 2.2 mm
- Lid screw bosses: 8.5 x 8.5 mm external bosses at Y +/-35.9 mm

Exported STEP files:
- exports/step/esp32_wroom_holder/erb_esp32_wroom_holder_assembly.step
- exports/step/esp32_wroom_holder/erb_esp32_wroom_holder_base.step
- exports/step/esp32_wroom_holder/erb_esp32_wroom_holder_lid.step

Assumptions made:
- Coordinate convention: X is holder width, Y is board length, Z is vertical.
- This holder is sized for the ESP-32S / ESP32-WROOM dev board in the supplied photos, not for the downloaded STL case geometry.
- The long side openings and bottom troughs intentionally leave the header pins reachable for IMU wiring while the board body remains protected.
- PETG-CF may reduce WiFi/Bluetooth range, so the lid has open slots above the antenna/module end.
- Print base flat on its bottom; print lid exterior face down or lip-up depending on preferred finish/support settings.
