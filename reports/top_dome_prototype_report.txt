Erb top dome prototype CAD report
=================================

Prototype intent:
- Visual/fit study for a removable R2D2-like hollow top dome.
- Not yet a final structural or sensor-mount design.

Final outer dimensions:
- Plain dome: 240.0 W x 240.0 D x 112.0 H mm
- Sensor mockup dome: 240.0 W x 240.0 D x 116.0 H mm

Design parameters:
- Dome outside radius: 112.0 mm
- Shell wall thickness: 3.5 mm
- Square mounting flange: 240.0 x 240.0 x 6.0 mm
- Flange holes: M4 clearance, 4.5 mm diameter, at X/Y +/-100.0 mm
- Rim skirt: 22.0 mm tall cylindrical overlap into the flange
- Sensor mockup front camera aperture: 26.0 mm diameter, no external pad
- Sensor mockup top cut: 48.0 mm diameter
- Top sensor boss: 64.0 mm diameter x 12.0 mm tall, embedded 8.0 mm into the dome

Exported STEP files:
- exports/step/erb_top_dome_plain.step
- exports/step/erb_top_dome_prototypes.step
- exports/step/erb_top_dome_sensor_mockup.step

Print/iteration assumptions:
- Intended print orientation is flange/open rim down on the bed.
- The hollow dome is a cover/sensor shell, not a load-bearing chassis part.
- The 240 mm square flange matches the current lower chassis top footprint for visual scale.
- The front camera feature is currently just an aperture; a real camera mount should be designed around the selected camera module.
- The prototype pair STEP is for side-by-side visual comparison only and is not printable as a single object.
